Our Mission
Transforming How the UAE Buys and Sells Vehicles
DUBIMOTORS was founded with a single mission: to eliminate the friction, uncertainty, and mistrust that has long plagued vehicle transactions in the UAE. We believe that buying or selling a car, boat, motorcycle, or jet ski should be as straightforward and transparent as any other major purchase.
By combining AI-powered tools with a curated network of verified dealers and private sellers, we have created a platform where every listing is trustworthy, every price is informed by real market data, and every buyer and seller has the tools they need to make confident decisions.
AI-Powered
Smarter Listings, Fairer Prices
Our proprietary AI engine analyses thousands of real UAE market transactions to give sellers an accurate price estimate before they list, and helps buyers understand whether a listing is priced fairly. Upload photos of your vehicle and our AI will automatically identify the make, model, trim, and condition — making listing your vehicle faster than ever.
Our Dubi Agent is available 24/7 to answer any question about listings, financing, regulations, or the buying process — in plain language, instantly.
