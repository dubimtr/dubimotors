5. AI Features and Your Data

AI Listing Verification

When you submit a listing, our AI system analyses the listing content to detect fraud, fake listings, wrong categories, and unrealistic pricing. This analysis is automated. Listing data processed through this system is not used to train our AI models.

AI Price Estimator

Vehicle details you enter are sent to our AI system to generate a price estimate. This data is processed in real time and is not stored separately from your listing data.

AI Description Generator

Vehicle details you enter are used to generate a listing description. The generated content is presented to you for review before publishing.

Dubi AI Agent

Queries you submit to the Dubi AI Agent are processed by our AI system to generate responses. These queries may be stored for quality improvement and abuse prevention purposes. We do not share your AI Agent queries with third parties for marketing purposes.

VIN Decoder

VIN numbers you enter are sent to a third-party VIN database provider to retrieve vehicle specifications. The VIN number is shared with this provider solely for this purpose.

6. Who We Share Your Information With

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Other users: Your name, phone number (if you choose to display it), and listing details are visible to other users
  • Service providers: Cloud hosting (Render), email delivery, payment processors, analytics providers, and AI API providers (OpenAI)
  • VIN data providers: Third-party vehicle data APIs used to decode VIN numbers
  • Law enforcement: Where required by UAE law or in response to a valid legal request
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition of DubiMotors

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained for the lifetime of your account, plus 12 months after deletion
  • Listing data: Retained for 24 months after a listing expires or is removed
  • Chat messages: Retained for 12 months
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by UAE financial regulations
  • AI interaction logs: Retained for up to 90 days for quality and abuse monitoring

9. Your Rights

Under the UAE PDPL and applicable law, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to object: Object to our processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for processing based on consent at any time
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@dubimotors.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, secure server infrastructure, access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

11. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@dubimotors.com and we will delete it promptly.
